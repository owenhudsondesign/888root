THE NOTCH

Nantucket Single Malt Whisky

Since its first release in 2008, The Notch has been recognized as one of the world's finest whiskies, earning dozens of awards, including: Gold Outstanding from the International World Spirit Competition, Best in Class from the International Spirit Challenge, Best in Class from the American Craft Spirits Association, Best in Class from the American Distilling Institute, Best in Class from the San Francisco World Spirit Competition, and World's Best Small Batch Single Malt from the World Whiskies Award.
